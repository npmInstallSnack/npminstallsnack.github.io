Interesting Food Info

  • Did you know? Honey never spoils. Archaeologists have found edible honey in ancient Egyptian tombs!
  • Apples float in water because they are 25% air.
  • Peanuts are not nuts—they're legumes! They're also an ingredient in dynamite.
  • White chocolate isn't chocolate, as it contains no cocoa solids.
  • Bananas are berries, but strawberries aren't!
  • Corn is a fruit, like tomatoes, watermelons, and even pickles.
  • Carrots were originally purple, not orange.
  • "American Cheese" is not American, and technically isn't cheese. It's a "processed cheese product" made of mixed cheeses and chemicals and is from English settlers.
  • Fruit Loops are all the same flavor, despite their different colors.
  • German chocolate cake is from Texas.
  • Nutmeg is a hallucinogen.
